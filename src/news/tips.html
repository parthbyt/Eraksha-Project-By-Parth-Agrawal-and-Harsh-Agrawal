Cyber Safety Tips

Interntet users:

  • Use a VPN, if you are sharing your info to a site which you not trust.
  • Take a speedtest, before going for e-conference, webinar, streaming etc. If your internet speed is slow, visit our speedtest page for tips on it.
  • Do payments and transfers on sites following "Hypertext Transfer Protocol Secure" protocol only. i.e. sites starting from https:// .

Computer tips:

  • Get an antivirus software if you are highly active online.
  • Keep applications updated for security reasons, use an updater service like Ninite or PatchMyPC for getting latest updates.
  • Use your operating systems disk utilities for getting better performance and getting rid of unnecessary files.
